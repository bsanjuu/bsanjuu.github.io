Since 1947

Primary School


Mpps Pothangal Kalan's location is at Gandhari, Nizamabad. 5 dedicated and professional teachers ensure that the children get the maximum out of their eduaction in this Other Board school. Mpps Pothangal Kalan was started in the year 1972. Telugu is the primary medium of instruction for this school and the student teacher ratio is 25:1. The school takes pride of its excellent teaching methodology. This school runs classes from 1 to 5 to provide education to students. This school has approximately 125 as student strength. It has a library as well with over 170 books. The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too. This school has 100% of students qualifying the examination and out of these 100% scored first grade.
